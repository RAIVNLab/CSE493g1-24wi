Deadlines and late days

Deadlines are primarily there to help the class run smoothly, such as turning in assignments and project deliverables in time. However, at times things are not in our control. To account for that we provide you with 5 late days that can be used at your discretion for any deadline. A maximum of 2 late days can be used for any 1 assignment. Weekends count as 1 day, so if an assignment is due on 11:59pm on Friday, it can be extended to 11:59pm on Sunday with one late day. After exhausting your late days, each submission will lose 25% credit for every extra day it is late.
